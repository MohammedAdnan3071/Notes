# On ramping 
In the context of building a financial application , 
"on ramping" referes to the process of converting traditional (fiat) currency into digital assets like crypto, tokens or credits that can be used within the application.

## Key Aspects of On-ramping :
1. Fiat-to-digital Conversion : Allowing users to deposit money using tarditional payment methods( eg bank transfer, credit/debit cards) and receive the equivalent value in digital assets.
2. User Verification : Integrating KYC and AML processes to comply with regulations , ensuring the legitimacy of the funds being converted.
3. Payment Integration : Supporting payment gateways or thrid-party services like stripe, paypal or specialized crypto-on-ramp providers.
4. Compliance and Security : Adhering to local and international financial regulations and securing sensitive data to build user trust.
5. Ease of Use : Providing a seamless and user-friendly experience for onboarding uses into the financial ecosystem, especially for those unfamiliar with digital assests.
## Example in Practice :
If you are building a crypto wallet, "On ramping would allow a user to ":
1. Link their bank account or card.
2. Deposit $100 into the app.
3. Receive an equivalent amount ( e.g 0.01BTC or 100 USDT) in their wallet.
