# H1  Authentation using Cookies

what are cookies ?
> 
Cookies in web dev are small pieces of data that a website stores on your computer or device when you visit it . These are used to remember information about you,such as :
 1. Your preferences : for example, a website might remember your language choice or theme settings.
 2. Login information : Cookies help websites keep you logged in without asking for your password every time .
3. Your activity : They can track what pages you visit or items you add you your shopping cart .
